About

Welcome to The Museum of Paleontology in Bucharest, a time capsule to the prehistoric world. Established in 1950, this remarkable institution houses an unparalleled collection of dinosaur fossils, gathered from across the globe.

Step into our halls and be transported back millions of years. Marvel at the towering skeletons of Tyrannosaurus rex, Triceratops, and Plesiosaurs, meticulously preserved and expertly restored.

For a limited time, our museum proudly presents the world's most complete dinosaur fossils, never before seen by the public. Walk among ancient giants and experience the sights and sounds of prehistoric landscapes firsthand.

Whether you're a seasoned paleontologist or a curious enthusiast, The Museum of Paleontology promises a journey through time that will leave you awe-struck and inspired.
